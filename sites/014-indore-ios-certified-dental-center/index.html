Our Specialities

World-Class Dental & Eye Care

From routine check-ups to complex procedures — our specialists deliver precision care with the latest technology, keeping your comfort first.

🦷

Root Canal Treatment

Modern, painless root canal procedures using rotary endodontics and apex locators. We eliminate infection at the root without discomfort, preserving your natural tooth and preventing extraction. Most cases are completed in a single visit with our advanced equipment.

✨

Teeth Whitening

Achieve a radiant, confident smile with our professional in-chair whitening systems. We use FDA-approved hydrogen peroxide gels activated by LED technology, lifting years of staining from coffee, tea, and tobacco in under an hour — safely and comfortably.

🔩

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with titanium implants that look, feel, and function exactly like natural teeth. Our implants have a 97% success rate and are designed to last a lifetime. 3D digital planning ensures precise placement for the best aesthetic and functional outcome.

😁

Braces & Clear Aligners

Straighten your smile with traditional metal braces or virtually invisible clear aligners. Our orthodontic treatment is personalized with digital scanning — no messy impressions. Clear aligners are removable, comfortable, and perfect for adults and professionals.

🩺

Painless Extraction

When a tooth cannot be saved, our gentle extraction technique ensures the procedure is quick and virtually painless. We use premium local anesthetics, atraumatic instruments, and follow-up care guidelines so you heal fast with minimal swelling or discomfort.

🌟

Scaling & Cleaning

Professional scaling and polishing removes years of tartar buildup, calculus, and surface stains that regular brushing misses. Our ultrasonic scaler is gentle on gums while thoroughly cleaning below the gumline, preventing gum disease and freshening your breath significantly.

👁️

LASIK Vision Correction

Freedom from glasses and contact lenses with our bladeless LASIK surgery. Using the latest excimer laser technology, we reshape your cornea in under 10 minutes per eye — permanently correcting myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Most patients achieve 6/6 vision by the next morning.

👓

Spectacle Prescription

Comprehensive eye examination using computerized autorefractors and manual retinoscopy for the most accurate spectacle and contact lens prescriptions. We test for near vision, distance vision, colour vision, and screen-related eye strain — helping you see life in perfect clarity.

🔬

Cataract Surgery

Restore crystal-clear vision with phacoemulsification — the gold standard micro-incision cataract surgery. We use foldable intraocular lenses (IOLs) customized to your lifestyle. The procedure takes 15 minutes, requires no stitches, and has an exceptional recovery rate with minimal downtime.

🩻

Retina Examination

Early detection of diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, retinal detachment, and glaucoma using dilated fundus examination and OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography). Regular retina check-ups are critical for diabetics and those above 40 — we recommend annual screenings for high-risk patients.

World-Class Facilities

We've invested in the best equipment so you receive accurate diagnoses, faster procedures, and more comfortable outcomes.

🩻

Digital Dental X-Ray

RVG (Radiovisiography) digital X-rays provide instant, high-resolution images with 90% less radiation than traditional X-rays. Better diagnosis, faster treatment planning.

🧼

Class-B Sterilization

All instruments are sterilized using Class-B autoclaves with biological indicators tested weekly. Single-use disposables wherever applicable. Strict infection control protocols followed.

❄️

AC Comfortable Rooms

All treatment rooms are fully air-conditioned, maintained at 22°C, with soft lighting and calming décor. Entertainment screens available to keep patients relaxed during procedures.

🚨

Emergency Dental Care

Toothache at midnight? Broken tooth before a wedding? We provide same-day emergency dental appointments. Call our emergency line and get seen within 2 hours, 7 days a week.

🔬

OCT Eye Scanner

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides non-invasive, high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina, detecting conditions like macular degeneration years before symptoms appear.

💻

3D Smile Design

Using digital intraoral scanners and smile simulation software, see a virtual preview of your smile after treatment — before you commit. Perfect for implants, veneers, and orthodontics.

♿

Wheelchair Accessible

Our clinic is fully accessible for patients with mobility challenges, including ramp entry, accessible washrooms, and wheelchair-compatible dental chairs for elderly and differently-abled patients.

💳

Flexible Payment & EMI

We accept all major cards, UPI, and net banking. EMI options available on treatments above ₹5,000 through our banking partners — no-cost EMI up to 12 months on select procedures.
