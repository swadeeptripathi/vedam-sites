Our Edge

Why Students Choose Lakshya

We've spent 10 years figuring out what actually works. These four pillars are why our selection rate stays above 95%.

👥
Small Batches — Max 35 Students
No 200-seat lecture halls. Every batch is capped at 35 students so every child gets individual attention. Doubt sessions are personal — not lost in a crowd. Teachers know your name, your weak areas, and your potential.
🎯
Daily Doubt Resolution
Questions don't wait. Neither do we. Daily 1-hour dedicated doubt sessions after every class. WhatsApp doubt group with faculty response within 2 hours. No student should ever sleep with an unsolved doubt.
📊
All-India Test Series
Weekly subject tests, monthly full-syllabus mocks, and our All-India Test Series with detailed analytics. Know exactly where you stand nationally. Percentile tracking, error analysis, and personalized improvement reports.
📚
In-House Study Material
Our faculty has spent years distilling the perfect set of notes, DPPs (Daily Practice Problems), and formula sheets. No need to buy 5 books — our material is complete, updated, and proven. Previous year question banks with video solutions included.
🧠
Personalized Mentoring
Every student is assigned a personal mentor — a senior faculty or topper alumni — who tracks your progress monthly. Strategy sessions, motivational check-ins, and career counselling are part of the program, not extras.
🏆
Scholarship & Merit Recognition
We believe financial barriers shouldn't stop brilliant students. Merit scholarships up to 75% fee waiver based on our entrance test. Monthly topper felicitation. Our top students also get placement assistance in hostels and boarding facilities near the center.
📍
Prime Indore Location
Located in the heart of Indore — near Vijay Nagar, easily accessible from all areas including Palasia, AB Road, and Ring Road. AC classrooms, fast Wi-Fi, a dedicated reading room, and a digital library with 10,000+ practice problems.
📱
Online + Offline Hybrid
Missed a class? Every lecture is recorded and available on our student portal within 24 hours. Online test series, live doubt clearing via Zoom, and parent progress reports — all available on your phone. The best of both worlds.
